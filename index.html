Welcome to SmartCookie Studying--a bite-sized, convenient study tool made for you!

Smartcookie Studying is a tool that allows you to set a study Pomodoro timer, create to-do lists, and more to enhance your studying experience!

Features: click to use!!

These features will all open a new tab; delete this home tab if needed, and use the "home" link on any page to come back!

  • Pomodoro Timer
    • --time management tool reminding you to take breaks. includes a to-do list!
  • Search Engine
    • --very basic, but will guide you to resources from great & trustworthy sources!
  • My Notes
    • --get access to my notes, including a search engine! I'm an 8th grader, so these are all 8th grade level notes, but they may help you too!
  • Gamified Flashcards
    • --enter in flashcards and play a reward-punishment based game with them
